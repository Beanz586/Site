Types of Beans

Kidney Beans

Kidney beans are a popular type of bean that is commonly used in chili and other dishes. They have a distinctive red color and a mild, slightly sweet flavor.


Black Beans

Black beans are small, oval-shaped beans that are commonly used in Latin American cuisine. They have a rich, slightly sweet flavor and are high in fiber.

Bean History

Ancient Mesopotamia

Beans have been cultivated for thousands of years, with evidence of their use dating back to ancient Mesopotamia around 7000 BCE.


Columbian Exchange

When European explorers arrived in the Americas, they brought with them new crops like beans, which were adopted by indigenous peoples and had a significant impact on their diets.

Health Benefits of Beans

High in Fiber

Beans are high in dietary fiber, which can help promote digestive health and support healthy blood sugar levels.


Lowers Cholesterol

Eating beans regularly has been shown to lower cholesterol levels and reduce the risk of heart disease.
